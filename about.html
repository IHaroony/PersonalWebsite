About Me

My Journey

Hello! I'm Haroon Younis, and my journey into software development started from a very different place. I began my career in communication, but found myself drawn to the world of software development. I've always been passionate about technology, and after working in customer support roles for some time, I realized I wanted to transition into something more technical.

That’s when I joined a bootcamp, which has been a game-changer. I’ve been learning new programming languages, tools, and best practices, while developing my skills through real-world projects. This journey has been both challenging and rewarding, and I'm excited about where it will take me in the future.

Why Software Development?

What excites me most about software development is the opportunity to create and innovate. I love solving complex problems and working on projects that have real-world impact. Whether it's building applications, developing APIs, or improving user experiences, I enjoy the creativity and logic involved in every step of the process.

My background in customer support has given me a unique perspective on user needs and how to improve products for better user satisfaction. Now, I'm applying that insight to software development, aiming to create applications that are not only functional but also intuitive and user-friendly.

The transition into software development hasn't been easy, but it's been incredibly fulfilling. Every day, I'm diving deeper into code, enhancing my knowledge of various programming languages, and taking on new challenges. It's a continuous learning process, and I'm committed to pushing my boundaries and becoming a skilled developer who can contribute to meaningful projects.
